What classifies an art peice?

A piece of art is something designed by the creator to specifically evoke something in the viewer, wether it be aesthetical or emotional, it generates a response from the viewer. It can be sculptures, music, sketches etc. All of the content you see below are art peices, wether they be 3d models or anything else, they all classify as an art peice.
